Refund Policy

Full Refund

Cancellations made up to 48 hours before the check-in date qualify for a full refund, minus processing fees.

Partial Refund

Cancellation made within 24-48 hours before check-in incur a 50% charge of the booking amount.

No Refund

Cancellations within 24 hours of check-in or no-shows are non-refundable

Early Departures

Guests Departing earlies than scheduled will be not be refunded for the reamning nights.

Special Promotions

Bookings under special promotional or discounted rates are non-refundable unless stated in the promotion terms.

Processing Time

Refunds may take up to 7-10 business days to process, depending on the payment method and financial instituion.
