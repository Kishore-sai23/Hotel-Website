FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What time is check-in and check-out?

A: Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM, and check-out is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request and subject to additional charges.

Q: Is breakfast included in the room rate?

A: It depends on the package you book. Please check the details displayed during the booking process.

Q: Do you offer airport transportation?

A: Yes, we offer airport pickup and drop-off services. Please contact our concierge desk at least 24 hours in advance to arrange this service.
